ABOUT ME

I have always been interested in the arts and yet interestingly today, one cannot separate the arts from technology. From 2015- 2017 I worked at the non-profit MACLA in San Jose, coordinating the youth programs and teaching underrepresented teenagers digital media literacy skills. Using digital media software to make digital arts and music sparked my curiosity in programming and development. I wondered how is this software made? I began learning to code on my own. Eventually, I joined the Web Development Immersive at General Assembly in San Francisco. Currently, I am working on personal projects and taking a weekly course in Dev Ops to become certified in various technical skills. I have always had a great passion for creativity and problem solving; programing and technology bring both of those skills to the forefront. Making the world a smaller place and connecting humans through technology and art is really a part of who I am.
